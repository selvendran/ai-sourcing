Privacy Policy for Sourcing Engine Ingest

Last Updated: July 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Sourcing Engine Ingest ("the Extension") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it when you use our Chrome Extension.

2. Data Collection

The Extension assists with professional sourcing by extracting public professional profile information, such as Name, Job Title, Skills, and Professional Experience, when you activate the extension on supported professional networking platforms.

3. How We Use Data

The data is used solely to assist you in organizing and managing recruitment leads within your private interface. We do not sell your data, and we do not use user data for advertising purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Data ingested through the Extension is transmitted to our secure cloud storage to facilitate your personal search index. We employ industry-standard encryption measures to protect your information.

5. User Rights

You maintain full control over your data. You may request the deletion of your ingested profile data at any time by contacting us at selvendrankkl@gmail.com.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact selvendrankkl@gmail.com.
